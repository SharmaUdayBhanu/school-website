About Us

Welcome to Dayanand Model School


Dayanand Model School Kartarpur warmly welcomes you! We are a renowned institution dedicated to providing exceptional education since 1965. At Dayanand, we believe in fostering a nurturing and stimulating learning environment that empowers students to develop intellectual curiosity, academic excellence, and strong ethical values. As the Sanskrit shloka "विद्या ददाति विनयम्" (Knowledge Imparts Humility) suggests, we strive to equip our students with the knowledge and wisdom to become responsible and compassionate members of society.
